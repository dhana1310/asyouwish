How it all started!!

AS YOU WISH>- it was never planned or I would say that I was never prepared for a career like this.I never harboured any such dream to be an Entrepreneur.Its DESTINY. We often tend to forget or the better word for it would be IGNORE certain extincts that happen in life.But I feel that those small or little ignorant things play its role.Well it all started in the year 2011 when I was in class10.It was my birthday and one of my friend-Shibangi Jaiswal surprised me with personalised cake baked by her.I was in Awhh for that particular moment.Like it was like,it wasn’t planned or decided.It caused to happen suddenly.The concern of her for me that encouraged her to woke up early in the morning and then bake a personalised cake and bring to school was simply Amazing and Especial. It was from then “Entrepreneur Keeda “struck my mind. I then started to think extremely strong about customisation of products.Then Boards happen and college started off.Back in 2015 I started off with my Business Idea .I was so strongly convinced and dedicated by the business idea that I started bunking few lectures and go for research work.I did a lot of research work .Then I took a loan of 30k from my dad to start off,and AS YOU WISH happened. MOTTO of it is to deliver as per customer need and requirement.This is something which I feel strongly about and passionate about.Today anything and everything that we do with an intention of making the person feel especial is in itself special .But adding a customised or personalised touch is just like adding cherry on cake .Gifting is common and also an essentiality requirement if we see in todays world.Everywhere or anywhere we go,be it friends family or relatives we tend to offer them a gift .So why not make gifting into personalised gifting hubfor everybody at an affordable and hassle free way.
